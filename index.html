♠ BlackJack

  • Dealer shuffle deck
  • Dealer deals 2 cards per player
  • Dealer declares win/loss/players out of game
  • One at a time, players in the game can either (repeatable)
    • Keep the cards they have or
    • Get dealt another card
    • Dealer declares win/loss/players out of game
